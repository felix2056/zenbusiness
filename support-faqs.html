Top 5 Customer Success Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my orders, documents, and information?

We have created one easy-to-use dashboard that stores your orders, business documents, and a record of your business entity information. You can sign in to your dashboard at any time to find the information you need.

How long until my order is complete?

We provide your estimated formation date both at checkout and in your dashboard. We do our best to complete your formation on or before this day, but there are times when the State or circumstances outside our control can slow things down.

How do I cancel my account?

There are no fees or penalties for canceling your account with us. If you would like to cancel your account with us, you can email us or call us directly at 1-844-493-6249 during normal business hours on Monday – Friday from 8 am – 8 pm Central time or Sunday from 10 am – 7 pm Central time.

What is a Registered Agent and how does the service work?

We offer registered agent services in all 50 states plus DC. Your registered agent will be available to receive official state correspondence for you during business hours. If you cancel this service, then you will need to show proof from the state that either our name has been removed as your agent or that the business has been dissolved.

*Note: In Maryland, the registered agent needs to sign the Dissolution and agree to act as the registered agent for 1 year after a Dissolution is filed.

What do I need to do for ongoing state compliance?

Our Worry Free Compliance Guarantee handles your annual filing, covers two yearly amendments, and ensures your company stays in good standing with the State while the Worry Free Compliance is active. If you choose to do it yourself, then the responsibility of ongoing state compliance will fall on you.
